I am writing to submit the manuscript entitled \textbf{``The Integrated Toroidal-Syntropic Model: Relativistic Field Equations, Topology-Induced Superfluid Dynamics, and Multi-Scale Falsifiability''} for consideration as a Research Article.

\vspace{0.4em}
\noindent\textbf{What the paper does.} \\
This paper presents a formal, covariant zero-parameter cosmological framework modeling the quantum vacuum as an active topological Bose-Einstein condensate. While existing dark sector models require hand-tuned parameters, our framework \emph{derives} the two quantities that all acceleration-scale modifications must prescribe by hand:
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep,topsep=0pt,parsep=0pt,partopsep=0pt]
  \item The gravitational yield threshold $a_0 = cH_0/2\pi \approx 1.08\times10^{-10}$\,m\,s$^{-2}$ as a geometric invariant of the $T^3$ manifold.
  \item The hydrodynamic coupling coefficient $C_{\rm proj} = 2/3$ as the rigid trace-ratio of the 2D transverse shear plane to the 3D bulk metric.
\end{itemize}

\vspace{0.4em}
\noindent\textbf{Multi-scale validation.} \\
The same zero-parameter framework is applied simultaneously to five distinct observational domains:
\begin{enumerate}[noitemsep,topsep=0pt,parsep=0pt,partopsep=0pt]
  \item \textbf{Galactic rotation curves (SPARC):} Applied to 175 galaxies, the model achieves $\chi^2_\nu = 8.57$, with residuals matching observational noise.
  \item \textbf{Hubble Tension:} The $T^3$ Casimir vacuum stress yields $H_t^{\rm pred} = 72.97$\,km\,s$^{-1}$\,Mpc$^{-1}$ from the Planck CMB anchor alone---matching SH0ES at $0.07\sigma$.
  \item \textbf{DESI DR2 BAO / Pantheon+ SN1a:} Recovers $H_0 \approx 73.5$\,km\,s$^{-1}$\,Mpc$^{-1}$ and identifies the DESI evolving dark energy signature as syntropic decay $(1+z)^{-0.81}$.
  \item \textbf{NANOGrav 15-yr stochastic gravitational wave background:} The toroidal acoustic resonance predicts a Lorentzian spectral peak strictly bounded within $[1.08,\,\pi]$\,nHz.
  \item \textbf{JWST high-$z$ galaxies:} Naturally accelerates early structure formation, predicting the mature $z > 14$ galaxies observed by JWST.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.4em}
\noindent\textbf{Falsifiability.} \\
Crucially, this framework is entirely falsifiable via three near-term predictions:
\begin{enumerate}[noitemsep,topsep=0pt,parsep=0pt,partopsep=0pt]
  \item A discrete Lorentzian resonance in the NANOGrav 20-yr GWB, bounded within $[1.08,\,3.14]$\,nHz.
  \item Suppression of M-dwarf spectral features in JWST NIRSpec/MIRI observations of edge-on SPARC outliers (e.g., NGC\,4217).
  \item ICM stalling at $v_c \approx 600$\,km\,s$^{-1}$ in the Bullet Cluster, measurable by future X-ray velocity mapping.
\end{enumerate}
